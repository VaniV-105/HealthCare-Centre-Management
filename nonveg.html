A Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan

  • Following a non-vegan diet plan for weight loss is right for your health as this type of diet provides your body
    with the required amounts of calcium, which helps in making your bones stronger
  • Non-veg foods are the primary source of iron and help in keeping blood deficiency at bay.
    Iron-rich poultry foods also ensure smooth functioning of the brain
  • Following a non-vegetarian weight loss diet plan will ensure that your body does not run a high
    chance of mineral or vitamin deficiency. Non-veg food items are a rich source of Zinc, Selenium and B vitamins.
    These minerals help in keeping you active throughout the day
  • The non-vegetarian food items are a rich source of protein, and therefore, it supports lean muscle gain.
    Eggs and other non-veg food items are great for speeding up the muscle recovery process of your body
